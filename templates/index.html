About Us

Join us at Bishoftu Conference Center, where innovation and collaboration thrive.

Welcome to the Bishoftu Conference Center, meticulously crafted by Group 6 for excellence. Situated in vibrant Bishoftu, Ethiopia, our venue blends modern sophistication with Ethiopian warmth. Our mission: to foster transformative experiences and meaningful dialogue.

With versatile spaces and a dedicated team, we offer seamless event solutions for conferences, symposiums, and workshops. Whether you're a global corporation or a local organization, our facilities and attentive service ensure your vision is realized with precision.
